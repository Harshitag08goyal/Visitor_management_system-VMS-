Welcome to Science Express!

Science Express is an innovative and mobile science exhibition aimed at creating awareness about science and technology. Our mission is to promote scientific temper and curiosity among students and the public. We invite schools to book a visit and experience the wonders of science firsthand.

Join us on a journey of discovery and ignite the scientific spirit in the next generation. Our mobile exhibition brings complex scientific concepts to life through interactive displays and engaging demonstrations.

About Us

The Science Express is a unique collaboration between various government departments and research institutions. The exhibition is hosted on a specially designed train that travels across the country, showcasing fascinating exhibits on topics ranging from biodiversity and climate change to physics and space exploration. Our goal is to make learning fun and accessible to everyone.

Since its inception, Science Express has covered over 100,000 km, visited more than 300 locations, and attracted millions of visitors, becoming one of the largest mobile science exhibitions in the world.
